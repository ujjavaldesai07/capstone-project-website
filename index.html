Cloud Connectivity Self Assessment
Project Description
Cloud connectivity self assessment is a tool to check whether customer needs a cloud connectivity support or not. The tool evaluates based on the responses given by the potential customer and also collects their contact information such as name, email and company name. This information allows Galen Data to communicate and discuss about their products with the potential customer.
